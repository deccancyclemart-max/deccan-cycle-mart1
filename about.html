Since 1989, we’ve helped over 1500 families experience the joy of cycling — starting with the first ride of happiness.

Our Story

Established in 1989, Deccan Cycle Mart is a family-run legacy built on a simple promise: to deliver a tension-free cycling experience. From our base in New Bollaram, we have become a trusted partner for over 1500 families across Secunderabad, guiding them from a child’s first tricycle to a professional’s performance bike.

Today, we are blending our three-decade heritage with a renewed professional focus. Our mission remains the same — to offer honest advice, reliable after-service, and access to the best cycle brands in the country. We believe that every cycle we sell is a smile delivered, and we are proud to continue that tradition.
